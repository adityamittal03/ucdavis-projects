Welcome!

Please fill out the form to get a prediction for the price of a diamond based on its characteristics. Use the helper guide for reference. Predictions from five machine learning algorithms are provided: Linear Regression, Polynomial Regression (Degree 2), Random Forests, XGBoosts, and an Artifical Neural Network.

Feature Descriptions

Carat: Weight of the diamond.

Depth: Total depth percentage. Computed as: z / mean(x, y).

Table: Width of top of diamond relative to Widest Point.

x: Length of the diamond in mm.

y: Width of the diamond in mm.

z: Depth of the diamond in mm.

Cut: Quality of the diamond's cut.

Color: Color grade of the diamond.

Clarity: Clarity grade of the diamond.

Importance of Diamond Jewelry

Diamond jewelry holds significant emotional and cultural value, often symbolizing love, commitment, and status Whether it's an engagement ring, a pendant, or a pair of earrings, diamonds are cherished for their beauty and rarity.

Additionally, diamond jewelry can be a valuable investment, with prices influenced by various factors including the diamond's cut, carat weight, color, clarity, and certification.
